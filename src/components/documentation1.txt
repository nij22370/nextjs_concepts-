   | **Feature**                    | **Example URL**                                      | **Search Params Used**      | **What It Enables**                                              |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Filtering**                  | `/products?category=shoe&brand=nike`                 | `category`, `brand`         | Filters product list by **category = shoe** and **brand = Nike** |
| **Sorting (Price Low → High)** | `/products?sort=price_asc`                           | `sort`                      | Sorts products by **price ascending**                            |
| **Sorting (Newest First)**     | `/products?sort=latest`                              | `sort`                      | Shows **latest products first**                                  |
| **Pagination**                 | `/products?page=2&limit=12`                          | `page`, `limit`             | Displays **page 2** with **12 products per page**                |
| **Search**                     | `/products?search=running+shoe`                      | `search`                    | Searches products by **name, description, or tags**              |
| **Combined Filter + Sort**     | `/products?category=shoe&brand=nike&sort=price_desc` | `category`, `brand`, `sort` | Filters Nike shoes and sorts by **price high → low**             |
| **Search + Pagination**        | `/products?search=hoodie&page=1&limit=8`             | `search`, `page`, `limit`   | Searches hoodies with paginated results                          |
| **Dynamic Deals**              | `/products?deal=today`                               | `deal`                      | Shows **today’s special deals**                                  |
| **Discount-based Deals**       | `/products?discount_gte=30`                          | `discount_gte`              | Shows products with **≥ 30% discount**                           |
| **Flash Sale**                 | `/products?deal=flash&time=active`                   | `deal`, `time`              | Displays **active flash sale items**                             |
